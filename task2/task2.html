Biography

Pawan Kalyan was born to Venkat Rao and Smt Anjana Devi in Mogalthur, Andhra Pradesh, India in 1965. Pawan has started an organization called Common Man Protection Force (CMPF) in October 2007. He has contributed 1 crore INR towards the corpus fund, CMPF aims to solve the social problems, help the neglected sections of the society in India.

He is the youngest brother of the Telugu actor Chiranjeevi and actor/producer Nagendra Babu.

Pawan Kalyan was first married to Nandini, but he left her and then started living with Renu Desai whom he worked with in his films Badri and Johnny. The couple has a son named Akira Nandan, named after his favourite director Akira Kurosawa.
